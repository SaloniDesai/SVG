Ike's - Late Night Diner [CLOSED FOR CONSTRUCTION - FALL 2013]

In the hub of President’s Park, Ike’s provides a late night dining option. Visit dining.gmu.edu for hours of operation.

Front Desk

In the event that a resident loses his/her dorm room key or access card, the Front Desk may provide a temporary key for 24(?) hours. The Front desk is also a source of information regarding what's going on in the park. You can also rent board games, movies, and sporting equipment from the Front Desk.

Lounge

The Lounge is a place where residents can meet, relax, and hang out. Here, residents can relax and watch TV together, or play a game of billiards, or just hang out.

Study Room

Here, Students will find a quiet room to study, open to residents any time of the day.
